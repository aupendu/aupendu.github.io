Biography

Aupendu Kar has joined the Department of Electrical and Electronics Communication Engineering of Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur as a Ph.D. Research Scholar in 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Prabir Kumar Biswas. Before that, he received an M. Tech degree in Instrumentation and Signal Processing from the same institute in 2018 and a B. Tech degree in Electrical Engineering from Kalyani Government Engineering College in 2015.

His research interests cover computer vision and deep learning, with special emphasis on low-level vision. Specifically, he is dedicated to designing learning frameworks by exploring the physical properties of various vision applications, including image super-resolution, dehazing, rain removal, lowlight, and underwater image enhancement. He is also interested in exploring learning algorithms for medical image analysis.
